Maharashtra

Maharashtra is a state in western India known for its diverse culture, history, and natural beauty. Here are some places that are worth visiting in Maharashtra:
Mumbai: This bustling metropolis is the financial capital of India and is known for its iconic landmarks such as the Gateway of India, Marine Drive, and the Taj Mahal Palace Hotel.
Pune: This city is known for its rich history and cultural heritage. It is home to numerous temples, museums, and historical landmarks such as the Aga Khan Palace and Shaniwar Wada.
Ajanta and Ellora Caves: These ancient Buddhist caves are located in the Aurangabad district of Maharashtra and are UNESCO World Heritage Sites. They are known for their exquisite rock-cut sculptures and architecture.
Lonavala and Khandala: These hill stations are located near Mumbai and are known for their scenic beauty, waterfalls, and trekking trails.
Mahabaleshwar: This hill station is located in the Satara district of Maharashtra and is known for its strawberry farms, panoramic views, and lush greenery.
Nashik: This city is known for its religious significance as it is one of the four cities where the Kumbh Mela is held. It is also famous for its vineyards and wine tours.
Tadoba Andhari Tiger Reserve: This national park is located in the Chandrapur district of Maharashtra and is known for its tigers, leopards, and other wildlife.
Shirdi: This town is known for the famous Shirdi Sai Baba Temple, which attracts millions of devotees every year.
