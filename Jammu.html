Jammu-Kashmir

Jammu and Kashmir is a union territory located in the northern part of India. It is known for its stunning natural beauty, rich culture, and adventurous activities. Here are some places that are worth visiting in Jammu and Kashmir:
Srinagar: This capital city of Jammu and Kashmir is famous for its beautiful lakes, Mughal gardens, and houseboats. It is also known as the "Venice of the East" and is a popular tourist destination.
Gulmarg: This scenic hill station is located in the Pir Panjal Range of the Himalayas and is known for its snow-capped peaks, skiing, and snowboarding. It also has the world's highest cable car, the Gulmarg Gondola.
Leh-Ladakh: This region is located in the northernmost part of India and is known for its rugged terrain, Buddhist monasteries, and adventure activities like trekking and rafting.
Pahalgam: This town is located in the Anantnag district of Jammu and Kashmir and is known for its stunning scenery, trekking trails, and outdoor activities like camping and fishing.
Sonamarg: This town is located in the Ganderbal district of Jammu and Kashmir and is known for its beautiful meadows, glaciers, and trekking trails.
Amarnath: This cave temple is located in the Anantnag district of Jammu and Kashmir and is one of the most important pilgrimage sites for Hindus. It is dedicated to Lord Shiva and attracts thousands of devotees every year.
Hemis National Park: This national park is located in the eastern part of Ladakh and is known for its snow leopards, Himalayan black bears, and other wildlife.
Zanskar Valley: This remote valley is located in the Kargil district of Jammu and Kashmir and is known for its stunning scenery, trekking trails, and adventure activities like river rafting.
